Context

This website was created as a college project by someone with limited programming knowledge and weeks of experience in web design. Please take the information here with whatever scrutiny you deem needful. While diligence has been taken to preserve data accuracy, this is effectively a work of passion in spare time. Please consider referring to the specs sheets themselves before making any decisions of importance. While here, please enjoy these generated images that I am allowed to use, as many of the pertinent photos and all of the important logos that will be referenced throughout the site are copyright protected.
